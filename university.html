UPM (Universiti Putra Malaysia) is a prominent public research university located in Serdang, Selangor. Recognized as one of the leading universities in Malaysia, UPM thrives on academic excellence and innovative research. Established in 1931 as the School of Agriculture, UPM evolved into a university in 1971. Its motto is "Berilmu Berbakti" which means "With Knowledge We Serve".

Why UPM Stands Out:

  1. Academic Excellence: UPM offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in agriculture, engineering, business, medicine, and environmental studies. It is especially known for its agricultural and biotechnology excellence.
  2. Research & Innovation: With a strong research-driven culture, UPM boasts advanced research centers focused on scientific, technological, and agricultural advancements - contributing greatly to national and global knowledge.
