Terengganu is a state located on the eastern coast of Peninsular Malaysia, along the South China Sea. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural landscapes, and beautiful beaches. Here's a bit more about Terengganu:

Geography & Climate

  • Terengganu is bordered by Kelantan to the north, Pahang to the south, and the South China Sea to the east.
  • It has a tropical climate with high humidity and significant rainfall, particularly during the monsoon season (November to February).
